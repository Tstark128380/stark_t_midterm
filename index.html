Cat Argument

In order to see why cats are superior to dogs in everyway, one must only look at their form

See the disinterested gaze, the tasteful black and white coat, the graceful silhouette created by the ears.
But if the perfect form is not enough to convince you just ask yourself "How often does a cat ever wanted to go for a walk?"
The answer is unknown because cats don't beg their owners for walks, they take themselves on walks.

thus the cat is easily a better pet than a dog.
